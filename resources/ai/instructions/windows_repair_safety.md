# Windows Repair Safety

Start with read-only diagnostics. Avoid repair, reset, delete, format, cleanup,
registry writes, service changes, and package removal until evidence supports the
action.

Before risky repair:

- Explain expected change.
- Capture relevant baseline evidence.
- Offer restore point when rollback may matter.
- Prefer scoped repair over broad reset.
- Verify original symptom and system health after repair.

Stop and ask human when data loss, boot risk, encryption risk, or conflicting
evidence appears.
